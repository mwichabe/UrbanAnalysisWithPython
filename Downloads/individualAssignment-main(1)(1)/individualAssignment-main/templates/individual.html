Triangle Numbers

Explanation

A triangle number, or triangular number, is the sum of the natural numbers up to a certain point. It forms a triangle when arranged in dots. The formula for the nth triangle number (Tn) is given by:

\[ T_n = \frac{n \cdot (n + 1)}{2} \]
